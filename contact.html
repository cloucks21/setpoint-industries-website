Start a conversation

The first call is sixty minutes. No pitch, no number.

Selling, or thinking about selling, the company you have built is not a decision made in a phone call. So we do not try to make one in ours. The first call is for us to understand the business you run, the customers who count on you, and what next looks like for you. If Setpoint is the right partner, we keep talking. If not, we say so.

What to expect

Two questions we will ask. Two we will not.

We will ask: what the business looks like today, and what you want next. That is most of a first call.

We will not ask: for financials, a valuation expectation, or an introduction to your broker. None of those belong on a first call.

Whoever picks up the email is the same person who would be at the closing table. No SDRs, no BDRs, no associate doing intake.
